Professional Accounting, Taxation, Audit Support and Business Advisory Services

Who We Are

Malik Associates (CMAs & CAs) is a professional accounting and advisory firm providing accounting, taxation, audit support, compliance and business consulting services to SMEs and corporate organizations.

We help businesses improve financial performance, maintain regulatory compliance and achieve sustainable growth.
